Risk Management

Risk management is the process of identifying, assessing and controlling threats to an organization's capital and earnings. These threats, or risks, could stem from a wide variety of sources, including financial uncertainty, legal liabilities, strategic management errors, accidents and natural disasters. All projects carry their own risks, generic and environmental. K2J manage it very efficiently by identifying the risks that the business is exposed to in its operating environment, Analyze the risk, Evaluate or Rank the Risk, Treat the Risk, Monitor and Review the risk
